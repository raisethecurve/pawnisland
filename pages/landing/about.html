About Me

Discover my journey in chess, faith, and community building.

Matthew Freeman

Hi, I’m Matt—founder of Pawn Island, a dedicated chess coach with over 20 years of experience, and an advocate for community building through chess and faith. My journey began under the mentorship of Mike Foycik, shaping my approach to chess and life.

I’ve coached players at all levels, led teams to national championships, and found joy in seeing students achieve their goals. Beyond chess, I’ve pursued passions in poetry, apparel design, and creating spaces for meaningful conversation and growth.

Pawn Island embodies values of integrity, creativity, and giving back. A portion of our proceeds supports local community initiatives, aligning with the dream my late brother and I shared of making a positive impact through our talents.

Accomplishments

  • 2024 Rhode Island State Chess Champion
  • Virginia State Grade 12 Chess Champion (2003)
  • Chess coach for Classical High School teams at nationals
  • Founder of Pawn Island Apparel, launched in November 2024
  • Published poet and creative community leader
