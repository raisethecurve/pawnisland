Doug's Story

Doug has been diagnosed with an extremely rare and aggressive cancer—Small Cell Colorectal Cancer, Stage 4. Only 17 people in the United States currently have this form of cancer, and Doug has been told it is terminal, with an estimated 3-6 months left to live. Despite enduring 10 rounds of chemotherapy, the cancer has metastasized and treatment options are very limited. Doug and his family are now seeking help through Dana Farber in Massachusetts, hoping to extend his life long enough to qualify for a clinical trial.

For nearly a year, Doug's family has worked tirelessly to care for him, facing significant financial stress from travel costs, medical care, and unpaid bills. With FMLA depleted and the risk of losing their home growing, every donation helps Doug's family regain stability and spend precious time together. Your support means Doug can continue to have his loved ones by his side during this difficult journey.
