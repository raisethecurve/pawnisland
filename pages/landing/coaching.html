Chess Coaching

Ready to take your chess game to the next level?

Whether you’re new to the board or aiming to break through that 1900 Elo threshold, our coaching programs provide a clear, proven framework to expand your understanding. As the 2024 Rhode Island State Champion, I’ve refined my methods through rigorous training — so you don’t have to wade through the endless sea of chess content alone.

Key Points:
  • Personalized Training: Online or in-person sessions tailored to your current skill level.
  • Expert Insight: Learn from a Champion who’s been trained by a Grandmaster and has coached teams that made it to nationals.
  • Holistic Growth: Improve decision-making, strategic thinking, and self-confidence — on and off the board.

Book an introductory lesson or contact us to set up a chess camp near you. Let’s cut through the noise and get you playing at your best.

Online Coaching

Stay Sharp from Anywhere

Our online coaching sessions bring the power of personalized instruction straight to your screen. Whether you’re an absolute beginner or polishing up a near-master-level game, I’ll tailor each lesson to your strengths and weaknesses—no cookie-cutter approach here. As the 2024 Rhode Island State Champion trained by a Grandmaster, I’ll guide you step-by-step toward new insights, improved tactics, and a more intuitive feel for the board. All from the comfort of your home.

In-Person Coaching

Hands-On Guidance for Real-World Results

If you’re within a reasonable distance, let’s connect face-to-face. In-person sessions offer an immersive experience, where we can engage in live analysis, practice with physical boards and pieces, and develop a deeper rapport. With direct feedback and real-time adjustments, you’ll see tangible growth in your chess understanding. From honing your opening repertoire to mastering endgames, I’ll help you unlock your full potential—one well-placed move at a time.

Group Coaching

Learn Together, Grow Together

Gather your friends, classmates, or colleagues for a dynamic group coaching experience. These sessions balance individual improvement with the camaraderie of shared learning. Challenge each other, compare strategies, and benefit from collective problem-solving. Group coaching is not only cost-effective, but also perfect for bonding and team-building—whether you’re a handful of chess enthusiasts or a workgroup looking to sharpen decision-making skills.

Chess Bootcamps

Accelerate Your Skills with Intensive Training

Our Chess Bootcamps are an immersive deep-dive into the game, designed for players who crave rapid improvement. Across a set timeframe—whether it’s a weekend workshop or a week-long intensive—you’ll tackle openings, middlegame strategy, tactical drills, and crucial endgame principles. Prepare for a challenging yet incredibly rewarding experience, led by a proven competitor who understands how to push you to your limits and beyond.

Scholastic Coaching

Empowering the Next Generation of Thinkers

From after-school programs to varsity-level training, I specialize in developing chess talent among students of all ages. My scholastic coaching sessions help young minds build discipline, strategic thinking, and confidence—on and off the board. Whether you’re forming a new chess club or gearing up for statewide or national tournaments, I’ll bring tailored instruction and a passion for mentorship that inspires academic achievement and personal growth.

Detailed Services

Explore Every Option for Your Best Game

Curious about individualized study plans, tournament prep, or specialized workshops? Need guidance on setting up a chess program at your school or workplace? Under Detailed Services, you’ll find all the extras that can make your chess journey complete. From one-time consultation calls to ongoing mentorship packages, we’ll customize a service plan that aligns perfectly with your goals, schedule, and ambitions.
